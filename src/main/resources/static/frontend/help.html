Understanding the Dashboard

Navigation Sidebar

The left sidebar contains links to all main sections: Dashboard, Channels, Analytics, and Help. Your profile menu is at the bottom with logout option.

Schedule New Posts

Click the "Schedule Post" button in the top right. Fill in your post content, select media, choose platform, and pick your scheduled time. Posts can be scheduled across any connected platform.

View Your Posts

Switch between "Scheduled Posts" and "Completed Posts" tabs on the Dashboard to see upcoming and past posts. Click "View Details" on any card to see full information.

Manage Channels

Go to "Channels" to connect your social media accounts. Click on a channel and follow the OAuth authentication process to connect your account. Each platform appears in a colored card with its status.

View Analytics

The "Analytics" page shows you detailed insights with professional charts displaying post trends over time and distribution across platforms. Statistics cards show total posts, active platforms, and pending posts.

Best Practices

Plan Ahead: Schedule posts several days or weeks in advance. This helps you maintain consistency and stay organized.

Know Your Audience: Schedule posts at times when your audience is most active on each platform.

Use Media: Posts with images or videos get better engagement. Always add relevant media when possible.

Check Before Scheduling: Review post content and preview before scheduling to avoid typos or errors.

Track Performance: Regularly check Analytics to understand what content performs best on each platform.

Calendar Tips

Monthly Planning: Use calendar view to see your entire month's content at a glance and identify gaps in your posting schedule.

Balance Distribution: Aim for consistent post distribution throughout the month. Avoid clustering too many posts on certain days.

Plan Ahead: Schedule posts for the upcoming month at least a week in advance to stay organized and reduce last-minute stress.

Update Schedule: You can edit or delete scheduled posts anytime before they're posted. Use the calendar to quickly find posts you need to modify.

Review Before Posting: Take time to review scheduled posts the day before they're posted to ensure all content is accurate and appropriate.

Quick Tips for Maximum Productivity

Batch Create: Schedule multiple posts at once in a single session. This is more efficient than scheduling one post at a time.

Platform Specific: Customize content for each platform. What works on LinkedIn may not work on TikTok. Tailor your message.

Optimal Times: Post when your audience is most active. Morning (7-9 AM) and evening (7-9 PM) are usually best for most platforms.

Content Calendar: Keep a separate document with your content ideas. This helps you plan posts faster in Graphura.

Use Links Wisely: Include links to drive traffic, but don't overuse them. Balance promotional and organic content (80/20 rule).

Advanced Features & Techniques

Hashtag Strategy: Use 5-10 relevant hashtags on platforms like Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn. Research trending hashtags in your niche for better reach.

Cross-Platform Posting: Schedule the same post to multiple platforms but customize captions for each platform's audience and style.

A/B Testing: Schedule similar posts at different times to see which performs better. Use Analytics to track results.

Call-to-Actions: Include clear CTAs in posts (e.g., "Like & Share", "Comment Your Thoughts", "Click Link"). This increases engagement.

Content Repurposing: Reuse successful posts in different formats or at different times to maximize ROI on your content creation.
